What are Beautify LA events?

does your business have a unique experience or offer they would like to share with the community?

Beautify LA started as an effort to bring the local community together. our goal is to help develop long lasting relationships between consumers and local businesses, large and small, by offering exclusive offers and unique experiences at thousands of locations. to start an event with the Beautify LA event platform you must offer something unique like and experience, or maybe an offer. we know many businesses have their own rythm so we leave the details up to you.
